Our School

Here at Rice Secondary, we keep our standards high and formal in an environment where every student is encouraged to excel academically, socially, and creatively. Our dedicated staff work tirelessly to provide guidance and support to ensure each student meets not only their personal goals, but also contributes positively to the community. We focus on strong discipline, respect, and innovation and aim to prepare our students for success both in further education and in life beyond the classroom.
