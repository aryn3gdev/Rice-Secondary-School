Our Rules

At Rice Secondary, our rules and standards are as follows,

1. Respect.
Our students are expected to be polite to all staff, visitors and fellow students, listen when others speak, respect opinions, backgrounds, beliefs and follow instructions given by our staff.

2. Behaviour. Our students are expected to behave in a mature, responsible manner at all times. They must follow staff instructions immediately*, move calmly and safely around school, avoid disrupting lessons and represent the school positively on both inside and outside school grounds.

3. Attendance, Punctuality & Equipment.
Our students are expected to attend all classes regularly, on time with proper equipment or provide a not or valid reason of absence.

4. Learning Environment.
Our students are expected to maintain a focused learning environment by completing homework on time, participating actively and respectfully in lessons and avoid any behaviour that disrupts lessons.

5. Safety.
Our students are expected to follow all health and safety instructions, avoid dangerous or reckless behaviour, report any safety concerns to a member of staff and exit the building in a calm manner during an emergency without running.

6. Use of Technology.
Our students are expected to use technology responsibly by following all online laws, avoiding accessing inappropriate websites or content and respecting others privacy online.

7. School Property.
Our students are expected to respect the school equipment, resources and building by keeping classrooms and corridors tidy, avoiding any damage to school property and taking responsibility for borrowed equipment.

8. Uniform.
Our students are expected to wear the correct school uniform at all times unless instructed otherwise. Uniforms should be clean and worn neatly and correctly in a professional manner.

We strive to uphold the following standards:
Respect
Responsibility
Integrity
Excellence
And we encourage parents/carers to inform their children of the standards and encourage them to follow it.
